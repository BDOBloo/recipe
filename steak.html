Juicy Steak on The Stove

This recipe will have the whole neighborhood hootin' and hollerin' for some more of your juicy meats!

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Red Wine
  • 1 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1 Sprig Fresh Rosemary
  • 4 New York Steaks
  • Some of Bloo's Secret Butter! Don't worry that secret recipe is listed here!

Alrighty! Now that the shopping is out the way, lets get down to business!

Directions

  1. Mix Red Wine with Salt and Rosemary in a small bowl
  2. Let stand at room temperature for 2-3 Hours.
  3. Heat a large griddle or cast-iron skillet over high heat.
  4. Place steaks on the hot pan and cook for about 4 minutes per side, or to desired degree of doneness.(Internal Temp should be atleast 145 Degrees F.
  5. Pour in the wine mixure, and allow to boil for a minute.
  6. Serve steaks with sauce on a deep platter.
  7. Enjoy!
