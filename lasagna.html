Lasagna

This mouthwatering delicious lasagna will have everybody in the household screaming for more!

Follow the directions properly and even Garfield will appear begging for some!

Ingredients

  • 16 Ounce Package of Lasagna Noodles
  • 1 Pound Lean Ground Beef
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • 16 Ounce Jar Spaghetti Sauce
  • 1 Clove Garlic, MINCED
  • 1/2 Pound Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
  • 1/2 Pound Shredded Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 Pint Ricotta Cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat Oven to 350 Degrees F (175 Degrees C)
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
  3. Add pasta and cook for 8-10 minutes or untile al dente.
  4. Drain
  5. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, brown beef and season with salt and pepper
  6. Stir in Spaghetti Sauce and Garlic
  7. Simmer for 5 minutes
  8. In a medium bowl, combine Mozzarella, Cheddar, and Ricotta (Stir Well)
  9. ina 9x13 inch pan, alternate layers of noodles, meat mixture, and cheese mixture until pan is filled
  10. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  11. ENJOY! (:
