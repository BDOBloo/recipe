Secret Butter Recipe

This Butter is that good good! My special secret to making them steaks so juicy and delicious there will never be a need for steak sauce again!

Ingrediants

Please keep in mind that the amount of each are per steak you're making.

  • 2 Tablespoons of unsalted Butter p
  • 2 Teaspoons of Garlic POWDER NOT SALT
  • 2 Teaspoons of Italian Seasoning
  • 1 Teaspoon of Cinnamon
  • 1-2 drops of Vanilla Extract

Directions

  1. Mix in butter with the dry ingredients in a small dipping cup.
  2. After thoroughly mixing the ingredients together, add in your vanilla extract.
  3. Mix in Vanilla Extract so it's all mixed together for about 3-5 minutes.
  4. Cover tightly with Plastic Wrap and toss in the fridge for 1-2+ Hours.
  5. Toss on your steak when it's almost finished cooking and let it melt on the steak!
  6. ENJOY!
